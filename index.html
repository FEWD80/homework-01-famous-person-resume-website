the great

Don Quixote

I do not see the world for what it is and prefer
to imagine that I am living out a knightly story.

I'm a nameless hidalgo (member of the lesser Spanish nobility), almost 50 years old, who reads so many chivalric romances that I lose my sanity and decide to set out to revive chivalry, undo wrongs and bring justice to the world under the name Don Quixote.

I recruit a simple farmer, Sancho Panza, as my squire, who often employs a unique, earthy wit in dealing with my rhetorical orations on antiquated knighthood.
